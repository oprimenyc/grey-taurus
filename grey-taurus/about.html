About Grey Taurus

"Flexible support. Multi-state reach. Proven delivery."

  • Florida-based LLC
  • Commercial & nonprofit clients
  • Multi-state initiative support

Grey Taurus LLC is a Florida-based operations and logistics support company providing flexible program and project support services across commercial, nonprofit, and government initiatives.

Founded with a focus on adaptability, Grey Taurus serves clients who need reliable operational infrastructure without the overhead of a large internal team. Our multi-state footprint allows us to mobilize quickly and support partners wherever they operate.

Our Approach

We work as an extension of our clients' teams — stepping in where capacity is needed, staying as long as the work requires, and exiting cleanly when the mission is complete.
